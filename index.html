Xui Bloodworth

Frail looking feminine feline with porcilean like skin. Long silvery strands of hair cascading over the shoulders with some bangs hanging over pale blue eyes. The younger of the twins and of course the smaller and weaker one. Because of their fraility they tend to carry a chained scythe with them. A weapon they mostly use for distance but close up if they had to and one manipulated by energy and cosmic. Where the scyth is thrown the earth below it trembles capable of moving the ground. "They say dirt don't hurt, but I beg to differ." "I'm sorry? Were you low on iron? Well we will have that remedied shortly!"
